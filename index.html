Sanjay Santhosh

Sanjay Santhosh is a passionate HR entrepreneur and the visionary mind behind InnoGrow HR Crew. Born and raised in Thrissur, Kerala, Sanjay identified a critical gap in how businesses approached their people — and set out to change it. With a deep belief that great organisations are built by great people, he founded InnoGrow to bring innovation, empathy, and strategic thinking to the world of HR services. His mission is simple: help businesses grow by investing in the humans behind them.
